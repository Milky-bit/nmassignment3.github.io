Let us know the basic of Complex Numbers

Complex numbers can be used to solve quadratics for zeroes. The quadrartic formula solves ax2 + bx + c = 0 for the values of x. If the formula provides a negative in the square root, complex numbers can be used to simplify the zero. Complex numbers are used in electronics and electromagnetism.

In mathematics, a complex number is an element of a number system that extends the real numbers with a specific element denoted by i, called the imaginary unit and satisfying the equation i2 = -1 ; every complex number can be expressed in the form a + ib, where a and b are real numbers.
